Hexen: Deathkings of the Dark Citadel is an expansion pack for Hexen.
Version 1.0 was released without a SNDINFO lump, for assigning MIDI
tracks to the maps.  Without it, music will only be played from a CD.
The Steam release of this game is unfortunately stuck at version 1.0.
